Posterize Project

This composistion focuses on the use of font to create texture. The image is derived from the movie "Fight Club" and features the character of "Tyler Durden" played both by Edward Norton and Brad Pitt. This poster depicts Edward Norton and various quotes from both characters throughout the movie then overlayed with the posterize effect creating layering, texture and hierarchy.

Map Typography

This project focuses on the use of a plethora of type. It's meant to be more abstract than good design. The combination of words describes the moring journey to school down to the finest details. Some text patterns are set into paths to create shape and flow to the composition.

Beer Logo & Packaging

This is only a piece of the entire project, but this depicts a logo I created advertising a local brand of hefeweizen. The piece is entirely in German that includes the font that I chose used widely in Germany and the Lucida Blackletter is based of traditional German blackletter fraktur. The logo itself is vector and completely made by hand going from sketch to vector and then alignment to create a gestalt style to represent a crown for the main logo.

Trace Project

This was a very time consuming project where I took a real life photo and vectorized the entire image. Every part of the piece is a solid shape with no gradients. Definitely a skill builder for the pen tool in Adobe Illustrator. The colors were also sampled from the photo as well.
